REQUIREMENTS

In order to experience this book as intended your computer must meet the following requirements:

  • A touch tablet computer or laptop computer with at least 9-inch diagonal display size.
  • One of these web browsers: Internet Explorer, FireFox, Safari, Chrome, Opera.
  • This page should have candy stripes touching all broders. If a border is white your display is too small and may cut off some content. If a border is red your display is larger than preferred, but will still show all content.

OPTIONAL ENHANCEMENTS

  • Perform display color calibration if your browser supports color management. Ignore this if you don't know what it means.
  • Run the browser in full screen mode. Some browsers cannot do this.
  • Find a quiet room with a comfy couch and a stereo to play some classical music or straight ahead jazz in the background at low volume.
  • If you have a honey, view this with them.
