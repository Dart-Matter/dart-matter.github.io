This book is simply organized as a row of pages from left to right. From now on use the following actions to move around the book:

IF YOU ARE USING A TABLET...

Turn to the next or previous page by placing your finger on the screen and sliding it to the right or left an inch or two with a gentle flicking motion, as if turning the pages of a book. You can also turn pages by double tapping the left or right side of the window. Flick down to get the table of contents.


IF YOU ARE USING A KEYBOARD...

Turn to the next or previous page by pressing the right or left arrow keys in the direction of the page you want to see next. You can also turn to the next page by pressing the space bar.  Use the down arrow to get the table of contents.

Enough preparation. Turn to the next page and enjoy the views!
